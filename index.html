OUR STORY

Born Where Spices Live

NaturaDry was built on a single belief — that nature already perfected the flavour. Our job is to preserve it. Rooted in Surat, at the crossroads of Gujarat's trading heritage and agricultural heartland, we source directly from farmers in Mahuva and Bhavnagar.

Every batch is cleaned, precision-sliced, dehydrated at controlled temperatures, and ground to exact specifications — without preservatives, without fillers, without shortcuts.

OUR PROCESS

From Farm to Your Factory

8 precision steps. Sun-dried. Zero shortcuts.

1
Farm Sourcing
Direct from Mahuva & Bhavnagar farms. Bulk seasonal procurement.
2
Sorting & Washing
Hand-inspected. Damaged pieces removed. Washed with potable water.
3
Peeling & Slicing
Machine-peeled, sliced to 2–3mm for even drying.
4
Dehydration
Tray-dried at 55–65°C for 6–10 hours. Flavour and colour locked in.
5
Grinding
Pulverized to mesh 40–80. Uniform particle size for consistent results.
6
Quality Control
Moisture ≤5% verified. Color matched. COA issued per batch.
7
Packing
Moisture-proof laminated foil pouches. Nitrogen-flushed for freshness.
8
Dispatch
FIFO warehouse. Pan-India or export shipment with full documentation.
Every grain of NaturaDry powder carries the warmth of Gujarat's sun and the precision of modern science.
